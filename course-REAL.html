Prerequisites / 선수과목
  • Linear algebra and multivariable calculus선형대수학 및 미분적분학
  • Probability theory and statistics확률론 및 통계학
  • Introductory machine learning (CS 3110 or equivalent)기초 머신러닝 과목 이수자
  • Python with NumPy and PyTorchPython, NumPy, PyTorch 사용 가능

Advanced topics in machine learning with emphasis on probabilistic methods and deep generative models. Students develop both theoretical foundations and practical implementation skills needed for independent research.

By semester end, students will derive and implement probabilistic inference algorithms, train deep generative models, and critically evaluate current ML research papers.

확률 방법론과 심층 생성 모델에 중점을 둔 고급 머신러닝 과목으로, 본과목을 통해 확률적 추론 알고리즘을 유도하고 현대 ML 논문을 비판적으로 평가할 수 있습니다.
