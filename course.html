Course Overview

This course covers advanced topics in machine learning with emphasis on probabilistic methods and deep generative models. Students will develop both theoretical understanding and practical skills.

Prerequisites: Linear algebra, calculus, probability theory, and an introductory ML course (CS 3110 or equivalent). Programming in Python with NumPy and PyTorch.

By the end of the course, students will be able to derive and implement probabilistic inference algorithms, train deep generative models, and critically evaluate modern ML research papers.
